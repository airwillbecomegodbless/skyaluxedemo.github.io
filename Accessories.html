Roof Shelters for Sun Protection

  • ✔
    Shields from direct sun and UV rays
  • ✔
    Keeps your space cooler in hot weather
  • ✔
    Works in combination with glass or polycarbonate roofs
  • ✔
    Sleek, modern design that elevates comfort and style

Stay cool and comfortable even on the brightest days with our roof shelter system, designed to sit above your glass or polycarbonate roof. This extra layer provides powerful sun and heat protection, reducing glare and UV exposure while keeping your outdoor living area pleasantly shaded.

Unlike a traditional roof, this shelter is meant to be used primarily in sunny conditions. When it rains, simply rely on the glass or polycarbonate roof beneath it for full weather protection—giving you the best of both worlds.
